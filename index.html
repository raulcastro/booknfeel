RESTAURANTS

We simplify the reservations spreading the best brands among guests of hotels and locals of Riviera Maya and Tulum.

Axiote - Playa del Carmen

After a long journey and working behind countless kitchens, today Chef Xavier has reached the maturity and the command at Axiote, Mexican Cuisine, a place where he seeks to enhance the everyday flavours, to respect and support traditional and local products from all over Mexico

Le Chique - Playa del Carmen

Located in the Azul Sensatori Hotel of the Riviera Maya and 10 years after its opening, this restaurant has awards ranging from the “Best of the Best 2014 – 2016 and 2017” from the Travel + Leisure Gourmet Awards to the prestigious “AAA Five Diamonds Award” among many others.

Skylights - Playa del Carmen

The Platinum Yucatan Princess is a five stars hotel which has opened it's doors to customers who want to have a special dinner at Restaurant Skylights, enjoy a delightful show and end up a perfect night staying in one of it´s nest rooms for the perfect prince.

PuroBeach

Defined as an icon of wellbeing and sociability, our first Purobeach in the Americas opened in December 2017, ilocated on the rooftop of The Fives Downtown Hotel, in the Playa del Carmen's most exclusive zone.

Fuego en el Hotel Mahekal

Mahekal’s newest oceanfront restaurant and bar, Fuego, offers an ever-changing array of Mexican cuisine that is locally sourced from the surrounding area, in step with the seasons and tides. The “Tulum-style” wood-burning oven restaurant features “Farm-to-Fork” dining accompanied with a relaxed vibe in a simple, laid-back atmosphere.

Oh Lala - Playa del Carmen

The cuisine at Oh Lala! is a refreshing change from that of other, more conventional restaurants. Guests are treated like good old friends in a relaxing atmosphere coupled with personalized service and good food.

MAE THAI (antes Gluay Maai)

Thai cuisine is one of the most robust and flavorsome of all asian cuisines. A true delight o the senses, traditional thai cuisine is characterized by its blend of hot, sour, sweet and salty flavors. Also for its abundant use of vegetables, sea food, meats, fruits and nuts.

Casa Banana

Situated in the tropical paradise of Tulum, Mexico, our Casa is home to an exotic dining and craft bar experience created by incorporating the culinary techniques of our home country, Argentina, with Mexico’s abundant resources.

Quinta de Piedra

Restaurant with the art of Mexican cuisine. The fifth course dishes have been coordinated with Mexican roots with a modern touch which can be appreciated on the palate.
